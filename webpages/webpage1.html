The Positives of Gaming

Gaming often gets a bad reputation, especially from people not born of the technological generation, though believe it or not, video games actually possess both physical and mental benefits if played in moderation.


For one, high-pace video games improve reflexes and cognitive ability, most notably quick thinking. Online games encourage pro-social activity which, combined with other factors, boost the mental health of a person so long as correct gaming timeframes are followed.


Aside from that, people use the medium of gaming as a source of income! People create videos on YouTube and stream games on streaming services in order to generate a stable amount of profit to maintain their lives, while also having fun doing their work.


All in all, gaming not only benefits the average person physically and mentally, but also serves as a medium for others to generate revenue while also having fun. It's important to remember, however, that gaming is only good in moderation, and that there may be consequences in doing too much of anything.
